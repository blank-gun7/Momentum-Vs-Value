**"Should investors buy past winners (momentum) or cheap stocks (value)?"**

## üìä Our Two Approaches

### Approach A: "Two-Dataset Method"
- **Datasets:** Stock prices (2023-2024) + Fundamental ratios
- **Sample:** 122 stocks after merging
- **Momentum:** Actual 2023 price returns
- **Value:** P/E ratios
- **Outcome:** 2024 returns

### Approach B: "Single-Dataset Method"
- **Dataset:** Fundamentals only
- **Sample:** 2,217 stocks (18x larger!)
- **Growth proxy:** Earnings growth (not true momentum)
- **Value:** P/E ratios
- **Outcome:** Price volatility

## üí° Investment Strategy Recommendations

### For Individual Investors:
1. **Buy recent winners** - Top 20% performers from last year
2. **Avoid recent losers** - Bottom 20% performers
3. **Rebalance quarterly** - Momentum persists 3-12 months
4. **Don't rely on P/E alone** - It's not predictive

### For Portfolio Managers:
1. **Overweight momentum** by 20-30% in portfolios
2. **Combine with other factors** - Add quality, low volatility
3. **Monitor for crashes** - Momentum can reverse suddenly
4. **Account for costs** - High turnover strategy
